## Outreach Guardrails

- Do not buy backlinks, trade links at scale, or ask for exact-match anchor text.
- Use descriptive, natural anchors such as "ATS resume template", "resume checklist", or "NHS nurse CV checklist" only when they fit the surrounding content.
- Prefer resource pages, newsletters, forums, university career pages, professional communities, and career coaches where the linked page solves a real reader problem.
- If a site asks for paid placement, sponsored content, or undisclosed link insertion, skip it.
- Track outreach manually; do not automate comments or forum posts.
